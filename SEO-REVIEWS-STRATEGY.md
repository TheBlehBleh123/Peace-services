## Your Current GHL Automated Review Flow

Since you already have automated review request texts in GHL with technician incentives, here's how to optimize what you have:

### Timing Optimization
- **Send review request**: 2-4 hours after service completion (not same day evening)
- **Follow-up reminder**: 3 days later if no review submitted
- **Maximum touches**: 2 texts per customer (don't over-ask)

### Recommended Text Templates

**First Request (2-4 hours post-service):**
```
Hi {first_name}! Thank you for choosing Peace Solar & Window Cleaning today. 
We'd love to hear about your experience — it only takes 30 seconds: 
{google_review_link}

Thanks, Jack & Ben
```

**Follow-up (3 days later, if no review):**
```
Hey {first_name} — just a quick reminder! Your feedback helps other 
Coachella Valley homeowners find trusted cleaning services: 
{google_review_link}

No worries if not — we appreciate your business either way! 🌞
```

### What NOT to Do (Google & FTC Violations)
- **Never** gate reviews (ask satisfaction first, then only route happy customers to Google)
- **Never** offer discounts/incentives directly for leaving a review (FTC: $53,088/violation)
- **Never** ask for specifically "5-star" reviews
- **Never** have staff write reviews
- **Technician incentives** must be for getting reviews (any rating), not positive reviews specifically

## Multi-Platform Review Strategy

Google is dominant but declining (71%, down from 83%). Diversify:

| Platform | Priority | Action |
|----------|----------|--------|
| **Google** | #1 | Primary automated flow via GHL |
| **Yelp** | #2 | Add Yelp link to email signatures, website footer. Do NOT directly solicit (Yelp filters solicited reviews) |
| **Apple Maps** | #3 | Growing fast (14% → 27%). Add to follow-up emails for iPhone users |
| **Facebook** | #4 | Share review link in post-service Facebook messages |
| **Nextdoor** | #5 | Ask satisfied customers to recommend you on Nextdoor |

### Google Review Direct Link
Create a short link for your Google review page:
```
https://search.google.com/local/writereview?placeid={YOUR_PLACE_ID}
```
Use this in all automated messages.

## Review Response Strategy

### Respond to EVERY Review Within 24 Hours

**5-Star Review Response Template:**
```
Thank you so much, {name}! We loved working on your {service type} in {city}. 
It's homeowners like you who make our work so rewarding. We look forward to 
your next scheduled cleaning! — Jack & Ben
```

**Tips for responses:**
- Include the **service type** (solar cleaning, window cleaning, etc.) — this adds keyword relevance
- Include the **city name** — local keyword signal
- Keep it personal and genuine
- Vary your responses (don't copy-paste the same reply)

**Negative Review Response (if it ever happens):**
```
Hi {name}, thank you for sharing your feedback. We take every concern seriously 
and would love the chance to make this right. Please call us directly at 
(760) 299-5187 so we can discuss. — Jack & Ben
```

## Review Content That Helps SEO

Train your technicians to naturally encourage specific review content:

### After Service, Technicians Should Say:
"If you have a moment to leave us a Google review, it really helps us out. Feel free to mention what service we did and anything specific you liked."

### Review Content Signals That Help Rankings:
- **Service keywords**: "solar panel cleaning," "window cleaning," "bird proofing"
- **Location mentions**: "Palm Desert," "La Quinta," "Coachella Valley"
- **Detail and length**: Longer, detailed reviews carry more weight
- **Photos**: Reviews with photos are prioritized in Google's "Stories" format on mobile

## Competition Monitoring

Check your competitors' review profiles monthly:
- How many reviews do they have?
- What's their rating?
- What are customers praising/complaining about?
- Are they getting reviews faster than you?

Use this intel to differentiate. If competitors are slow, your velocity advantage compounds.
