## Content Guidelines

### Every Blog Post Must Include:

- 1,500+ words minimum
- 1 primary keyword + 2-3 secondary keywords
- 3-5 internal links (to service pages, location pages, other posts)
- At least 1 original photo or before/after image
- Clear CTA to get a quote
- Author byline (Jack or Ben) for E-E-A-T signals
- Schema markup (Article type)

### E-E-A-T Signals to Include:

- **Experience:** Reference real jobs, specific Coachella Valley conditions
- **Expertise:** Include data, measurements, efficiency percentages
- **Authority:** Cite manufacturer guidelines, industry standards
- **Trust:** Use real photos, real results, real customer stories
