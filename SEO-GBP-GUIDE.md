## 8. Business Hours

**Business hours are now a top-5 ranking factor** (Whitespark 2026). "Business is open at time of search" directly affects whether you show in the Local Pack.

Recommended:
- Set accurate hours (don't inflate just for ranking)
- Consider slightly extended hours if you're available (e.g., 7am-6pm instead of 8am-5pm)
- Mark holiday hours in advance
- If you take calls outside business hours, note "Hours might differ" and add special hours
